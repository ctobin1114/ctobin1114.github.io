Member Database Management & Automation

My senior capstone project for software engineering at UC Merced. Developed alongside Shelly Abu, Tyler Armstrong, and Derek Stanford.

A web app and database designed to replace the California Tow Truck Agency's (CTTA) previous providers with mild data migration.

Final product was presented at Innovate to Grow (I2G), UC Merced's school of engineering capstone showcase.

Utilizes SQLite for the database, Python and Flask as the web framework and backend component, and HTLM/CSS/JavaScript, JQuery, and Bootstrap for the frontend.
