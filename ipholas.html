Ipholas

My D&D 5e Homebrew Setting and Content

Ipholas is the setting of the D&D campaigns I am a Dungeon Master of. Along with the setting come homebrew mechanics, some of which I have adapted from some other DM's work.

Ipholas is a world of adventure, magic, and intrigue. Adventurers party in the streets of Stealaway. Aristocrats rule over the workers of Alarahn. Wannabe adventurers "slaying pigbats" in Leashire adventuring facilities. The United Cities triumverate rewriting its history. The cannibal tribes of the Southern Wastes.

There are plenty of stories to be told across the lands of Ipholas. On my World Anvil page, I chronicle the homebrew content, house rules, lore, and tales from the world of Ipholas.
